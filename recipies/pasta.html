Pasta recipie

Description

Here we are going to make delicius pasta using tomato,onions,garlic & other easily aailable ingredients

Ingredients

  • Pasta of any type
  • Tomato
  • onions
  • Coriander leaves
  • Oil
  • Herbs
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cook Pasta with little oil & enough salt
  2. Drain it & keep aside
  3. Saute other ingredients
  4. ONce cooked,Blend them to make a paste
