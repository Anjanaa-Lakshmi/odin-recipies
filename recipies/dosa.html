Dosa Recipie

Description

Dosa is the ever-popular South Indian breakfast of crispy crepes made with fermented rice and lentil batter. With just a handful of ingredients learn to make the classic Dosa recipe from scratch Dosa goes well with chutney or sambhar.A glass of chaya enhances the taste.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups Parboiled Rice
  • 1/2 cup Whole Urad Dal
  • 1/4 teaspoon Fenugreek Seeds
  • 1 tablespoon Cooked rice
  • Water, as needed
  • Salt to tast
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Rinse the Parboiled rice,Urad dal and fenugreek seed with water for 3-4 times & soak it in water for 4-5 hours
  2. Grind the soaked ingredients in the above step along with cooked rice & enough amount of water to make a thick batter.
  3. Store the batter at room temperature for 8-10 hours or overnight.
  4. Before making dosa,put required amount of salt
  5. Sizzle some oil on dosa tawa & start making delicious dosas
