Putt Recipie

Description

Lets make one of the most popular breakfast in Kerala.Really quick & one of the most go to recipie in most kerala houses,Putt is considered healty as it is steamed & no flavouring is added to it.Putt can be made using rice flour,red rice flour ,wheat flour or even ragi flour.You can also use readymade putt podi avaiable in market.

Putt also goes well with Chicken curry,beef curry,egg curry or even with black tea & little sugar(my go to side dish for putt).But putt & kadala is an iconic combination & it is even more tasty along with pappadam & small ripe bananas.

Ingredients

  • Rice flour 1 Cup
  • Grated Coconut 1/2 cup
  • Water as required (Will be mentioned in putt podi)
  • Salt for taste

Steps

  1. Add salt and water to the flour, mix well so that there are no lumps.
  2. Fill the mixture in puttu maker. First make a layer of grated coconut, then flour.
  3. Like this make alternative layers. Steam till cooked.
